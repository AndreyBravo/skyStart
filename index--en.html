Where Do We Come From?

Our history began in 2017 in Minsk, when a small team of friends started developing an online store builder. In 2018 Skystart company was founded.

In 2021 we have become a part of Aleph Holding — global conglomerate on a digital ads market, with offices in 72 countries, a strategic partner for Twitter, Facebook and TikTok.

Aleph Holding in numbers

Aleph is the parent company for Httpool, IMS, Ad Dynamo, Social Snack и Wise.Blue.
